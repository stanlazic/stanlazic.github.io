Stanley E. Lazic, PhD

After a decade working in the Swiss and British pharmaceutical industry, I left as an Associate Director in Statistics and Machine Learning to start Prioris.ai Inc. – a company focusing on probabilistic predictive modelling (aka Bayesian machine learning) for clients in the pharmaceutical, life science, and healthcare industries.

I have a PhD in Neuroscience and Master's in Computational Biology from King's College at the University of Cambridge and currently live in Ottawa, Canada.

I've written a book on experimental design and am working on a second about Bayesian data analysis for laboratory biologists. Research interests include the design and analysis of biological experiments, improving the reproducibility of biomedical research, and novel applications of Bayesian methods.

Publication list is above, professional information is on LinkedIn, and I tweet at @StanLazic.
