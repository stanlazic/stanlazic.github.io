Stanley E. Lazic, PhD

Originally from Canada, I moved to Cambridge (UK) in 2002 for a PhD in neuroscience and stayed for a postdoc and a masters in computational biology. From 2009 to 2016 I worked as a statistician/computational scientist in the Swiss pharmaceutical industry and in 2016 I moved back to Cambridge and am currently an Associate Director in Statistics and Machine Learning at AstraZeneca.

Research interests include the design and analysis of biological experiments, improving the reproducibility of biomedical research, and novel applications of Bayesian methods.
